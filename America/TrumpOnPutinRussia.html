An Encyclopedic Record of President Donald J. Trump's Public Statements on Vladimir Putin and the Russian Federation (1987-2025)

Introduction

This report provides a comprehensive, chronologically organized record of public statements made by Donald J. Trump concerning Russian President Vladimir Putin and the Russian Federation. The scope of this document extends from his early business career through his second term in office (ending circa 2025), as documented in the available source material. The objective is to present an unembellished, factual repository of President Trump's declared positions and personal assessments, intended to serve as a primary source reference for research, policy analysis, and historical documentation. Each entry is dated and sourced to ensure fidelity and verifiability.

Relative Morality: The Coordinate System

The Kanon does not use a binary "Good vs Evil" scale. Instead, it uses a Vector Field defined by two axes:

  • υ
    The Moral Vector (Benefit vs Cost) Who benefits? +1 (Universal Benefit) vs -1 (Self Benefit). Does this concept serve the whole or just the self?
  • ψ
    The Will Vector (Proactive vs Suppressive) What is the intent? +1 (Proactive/Create) vs -1 (Suppressive/Prevent). Is the energy flowing or blocking?

* A Concept is judged by its coordinates (υ, ψ). "Good" is typically High Benefit + High Creation (Greater Good). "Evil" is Self-Benefit + Suppression (The Void).

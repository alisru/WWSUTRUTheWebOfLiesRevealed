The Core Persona

The record that emerges across all seven planes is not a single monster and not a misunderstood battler, it's a Guardian pointed inward. Where the Kanon's ideal (the Stoic Guardian) holds the door open, this pattern holds it, watches it, and decides who gets to knock. She is not a conqueror and not a builder; she is a Sentinel, and the question that actually separates her HITs from her FAILs, plane after plane, is simply: who is the "us" she is guarding?
  • The Bond, Narrowed: She speaks fluent egalitarianism, the Battler, the Pub Test, the Fair Go, but the audit's own FAIL column shows the circle those words draw keeps a specific shape.
  • The Fortress, Not the Land: She adopts the continent's harsh, self-reliant mythology (distance, drought, "she'll be right") but converts it into a border logic rather than a stewardship logic.
  • The Grievance, Institutionalised: Her strongest plane (Drive, 38 HITs) and her weakest (Result, 21 HITs) are the same drive followed to two different destinations, real anti-elite anger that starts genuine and ends exclusionary.

The 7 Pillars of the Pattern

I. Identity: The Battler's Guardian (Who)

Plane 1 (Identity), avg υ: −0.14, ψ: −0.05 — 28 HIT / 25 FAIL
Selective Egalitarianism

She genuinely speaks the dialect of Australian egalitarianism, no one above her, no one below her, and a professed contempt for pretension that reads as authentic rather than performed. The audit's HIT column on this plane is real: half of Identity's 53 vectors show a person who actually distrusts hierarchy and actually claims to speak for the room, not above it.

The Bait: Battler, Pub Test, Larrikin → The True Intent: Exclusion, Alarmism

The same egalitarian vocabulary that wins her a HIT on "speaks for the ordinary person" wins her a FAIL on "defines who counts as ordinary." The Fair Go she invokes is structurally real, it is also structurally fenced.

The Practical Record

In practice this shows up as authentic-sounding plain talk deployed to argue that specific groups, First Nations Australians, immigrants, particular religions, sit outside the circle the plain talk is meant to protect.

III. Land: The Fortress Warden (Where)

Plane 3 (Land), avg υ: +0.01, ψ: +0.06 — 31 HIT / 18 FAIL
Border as Stewardship

She speaks the continent's real vocabulary: distance, drought, self-reliance, the "tyranny of distance" as lived experience rather than metaphor. Where the Kanon's ideal converts that vocabulary into ecological humility, her record converts it into a spatial logic about who is allowed to cross which lines.

The Bait: The Steward, Self-Reliance → The True Intent: Quarantine, Terra Nullius

Deep-time respect for the land is replaced, plane after plane, by a border-line reading of it, the same terrain, guarded rather than held.

The Practical Record

Policy focus consistently lands on offshore processing and border security framed as protecting national character, not on land or water stewardship in the Kanon's sense.

V. Method: The Improviser Split Down the Middle (How)

Plane 5 (Method), avg υ: −0.07, ψ: −0.06 — 23 HIT / 26 FAIL (closest to an even split of any plane)
Individual Yes, Collective No

Genuinely embodies the grassroots, direct-democracy, do-it-yourself side of the national method, the secret ballot's protective function and self-reliant improvisation both check out against the record. The pattern breaks cleanly at the point where the method requires a collective institution rather than an individual one.

The Bait: The Bush Mechanic, The Secret Ballot → The True Intent: Institutional Resistance

Individual-level mechanisms (referendums, secret ballots, self-reliance) consistently HIT; collective mechanisms (arbitration, bargaining, the institutions that transmit power between groups) consistently FAIL.

The Practical Record

Consistent support for the tools an individual can use alone, paired with consistent resistance to the tools that only work when a group uses them together.

VII. Result: The Paranoid Sentinel (Effect)

Plane 7 (Result), avg υ: −0.28, ψ: −0.11 — 21 HIT / 29 FAIL (her weakest plane by a clear margin)
Insularity as Security

This is where the whole pattern cashes out. The outcomes she actually argues for, immigration reduction framed as lifestyle preservation, insularity framed as security doctrine, opposition to the Voice and to native title, are the plane's clearest and most consistent FAIL column in the entire audit.

The Bait: The Fair Go → The True Intent: The Fair Go, Narrowed to the In-Group

Every plane's small, real HITs on genuine egalitarian instinct converge here into their final, measurable shape: an outcome record that consistently narrows rather than extends who the nation's protections reach.

The Practical Record

Consistent, documented opposition to the concrete mechanisms (the Voice, native title, regional partnership, immigration intake) that would extend the Fair Go outward rather than inward.

The Narrative of the Pattern

The Siege Matriarch stands on the same Verandah as the Kanon's ideal, looking at the same continent.

She is The Fortress Warden of the land, but where the ideal holds the land, she holds the line around it.

Bound by a real Battler's instinct, she judges people by their willingness to have a go, then narrows who's allowed to try.

Her strongest drive is genuine, The Anxious Battler's Champion in her speaks for real grievance before it hardens into exclusion.

She governs as The Improviser Split Down the Middle, trusting the individual completely and the collective barely at all.

She carries The Convict's Grievance honestly, then reads its inheritance more narrowly than the story itself requires.

Ultimately she is The Paranoid Sentinel, the plane where every other plane's small honest HITs converge into one measurable, narrowing outcome.

II. Definition: The Constitutional Disruptor (What)

Plane 2 (Definition), avg υ: +0.03, ψ: −0.01 — 33 HIT / 18 FAIL (her best-aligned plane)
Genuine Senate Leverage

This is the plane where the record is most honestly split in her favour. Her instinct for direct democracy, the value she places on the secret ballot, and her willingness to use minor-party Senate leverage as an actual check on major-party power are documented, real, and align with legitimate constitutional traditions rather than a corrupted version of them.

The Bait: The Constituent, The Compelled Voter → The True Intent: Gridlock as Strategy

The same leverage that produces a genuine check on power, on this plane, also produces a documented pattern of obstruction pursued as an end in itself rather than a means to a specific reform.

The Practical Record

Balance-of-power votes that block or amend legislation sit in both columns of this audit, sometimes a real constitutional check, sometimes obstruction with no stated policy destination.

IV. Drive: The Anxious Battler's Champion (Why)

Plane 4 (Drive), avg υ: +0.09, ψ: +0.05 — 38 HIT / 14 FAIL (her highest hit rate of any plane)
Real Grievance, Real Mechanism

This is the plane the whole archetype pivots on. Distrust of "tall poppies," suspicion of bludgers, a genuine self-reliance ethic, all of it tracks a real national drive with real mechanism, not a costume. Her HIT rate here is the strongest evidence in the whole audit that the persona isn't pure performance.

The Bait: Mateship, Solidarity → The True Intent: Solidarity for the In-Group Only

The same solidarity instinct that produces genuine mateship on this plane produces, on Plane 7 (Result), an outcome where that solidarity has a fence around it.

The Practical Record

Economic and social anxiety is consistently framed in defensive, protectionist terms, real grievance, aimed narrowly rather than channelled outward.

VI. Foundation: The Convict's Grievance (Cause)

Plane 6 (Foundation), avg υ: +0.02, ψ: +0.04 — 30 HIT / 19 FAIL
Selective Founding Myth

Draws convincingly on the rebellious, egalitarian founding mythology, the convict origins, the goldfields, distrust of imposed authority, and this connects to real anti-elite sentiment rather than an invented one. The audit's split verdict here shows a founding story that's honestly held and selectively applied.

The Bait: The Goldfields Rebel → The True Intent: A Narrower Reading of Who Belongs to It

The same founding myths that justify legitimate skepticism of concentrated power are read, elsewhere on this plane, to draw a tighter circle around who the founding story actually includes.

The Practical Record

Invokes origin-story language selectively, present when it argues against elites, largely absent when the same origin story would argue for a broader national inheritance.
